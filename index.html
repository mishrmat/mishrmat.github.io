About Me

My name is Misha and I live in Bellingham, WA. Im from Seattle, my favorite color is orange, my middle name is Roscoe, the last book I read was The Prophet by Kahlil Gibran, I can do a handstand, and if I were a dog Id be a Cairn Terrier. I am studying design at Western Washington University and am really enjoying it. I am liking branding and packaging most, though all of it has been interesting. I am also a Design Club officer, a DJ on KUGS 89.3 FM, a designer at the Publicity Center, and a desk attendant at the Viking Union Reservations and Access Desk. I have a lot of friends that I love very much- my favorite things to do with them are watch movies, hang out at the lake, and go to thrift stores and antique malls.
